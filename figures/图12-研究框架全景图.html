Framework Logic

This three-layer framework organizes the project into a logical progression: from a single, well-defined scientific problem (defect density scaling law), through three complementary research approaches (empirical characterization, formal ceiling, compositional breakthrough), to three targeted innovation contributions, each supported by specific experimental validation. The framework ensures research coherence while maintaining methodological rigor across the quantitative (RC1), formal (RC2), and algorithmic (RC3) dimensions.
