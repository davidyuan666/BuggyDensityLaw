Practical Scope of the Formal Defect Ceiling

Verification complexity follows its own scaling law: Z3 solving time grows as TZ3 ∝ LOC1.4 and NuSMV model-checking time grows as TSMV ∝ LOC2.1·2|Φ|. Crucially, the slope of verification time growth is shallower than the defect density scaling slope for practical code sizes (LOC < 500, |Φ| < 10). Under a 60-second timeout, Z3 can verify safety properties for code up to ~400 LOC, and NuSMV up to ~200 LOC with 4 properties. This defines the engineering trade-off boundary of the "Formal Defect Ceiling" concept and motivates the compositional strategy (Research Content 3) to keep per-module code within these feasible verification bounds.

Based on Z3 4.12 and NuSMV 2.6 benchmarks on industrial control-logic verification tasks.
