Exponential Advantage of Compositional Strategy

The compositional correctness strategy yields an exponential advantage over monolithic generation. With k=5 sub-modules and per-module verification pass rate p=0.85, the system-level correctness probability reaches 0.44 (vs 4.5×10-5 for monolithic generation at N=200 tokens, ε=0.05). The expected defect density ratio (monolithic/compositional) grows exponentially with k, matching BES Theorem 4.5's theoretical prediction of Ω(p-(m-1)/log m). This provides the mathematical foundation for our "breaking the scaling law" claim in Innovation Point 3.

Per-module size: n = N/k = 200/k LOC. Per-module verification pass rate: p = (1-ε)n.
